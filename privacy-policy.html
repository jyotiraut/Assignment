Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Krishi Dashboard ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application to view TikTok video analytics for an authorized TikTok account.

1. What We Access

With explicit authorization from the TikTok account holder via TikTok's official OAuth login flow, the App accesses the following data through TikTok's official API:

  • Basic public profile information (username, display name, follower count, video count)
  • Per-video statistics (view count, like count, comment count, share count, and post date) for videos belonging to the authorized account

We do not access private messages, payment information, or any data outside the scopes explicitly granted during authorization.

2. How We Use This Data

The data is used solely to generate analytics and visualizations (such as posting frequency, total views, average engagement, and growth trends) for the account holder who authorized the connection. We do not use this data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to providing analytics to the account owner.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share this data with third parties. Data is not used to train machine learning models and is not disclosed to any party other than the authorized account holder.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Data retrieved from the TikTok API is stored only as long as necessary to provide the analytics dashboard to the account holder. The account holder may request deletion of all stored data at any time by contacting us using the details below.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect any stored data and access tokens, including restricting access to authorized use only.

6. Your Rights

The TikTok account holder may revoke this App's access at any time through TikTok's account settings. Upon revocation, we will stop retrieving new data and will delete previously stored data upon request.

7. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children and is not intended for use by anyone under the age required to hold a TikTok account.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
